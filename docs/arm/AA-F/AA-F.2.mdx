<MarginText>8</MarginText>
<p>Max{"_"}Scale is the largest N such that 10.0{"*"}{"*"}(&ndash;N) is allowed as a decimal type's delta. Its type is <i>universal{"_"}integer</i>.<br /></p>

<MarginText>9</MarginText>
<p>Min{"_"}Scale is the smallest N such that 10.0{"*"}{"*"}(&ndash;N) is allowed as a decimal type's delta. Its type is <i>universal{"_"}integer</i>.<br /></p>

<MarginText>10</MarginText>
<p>Min{"_"}Delta is the smallest value allowed for <i>delta</i> in a <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-3/AA-3.5#S0049">decimal_fixed_point_definition</a></code>. Its type is <i>universal{"_"}real</i>.<br /></p>

<MarginText>11</MarginText>
<p>Max{"_"}Delta is the largest value allowed for <i>delta</i> in a <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-3/AA-3.5#S0049">decimal_fixed_point_definition</a></code>. Its type is <i>universal{"_"}real</i>.<br /></p>

<MarginText>12</MarginText>
<p>Max{"_"}Decimal{"_"}Digits is the largest value allowed for <i>digits</i> in a <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-3/AA-3.5#S0049">decimal_fixed_point_definition</a></code>. Its type is <i>universal{"_"}integer</i>. <br /></p>

<AnnotatedOnly>
<MarginText>12.a</MarginText>
<Admonition type="aarm" aarm="reason">
<b></b>The name is Max{"_"}Decimal{"_"}Digits versus Max{"_"}Digits, in order to avoid confusion with the named number System.Max{"_"}Digits relevant to floating point. <br />
</Admonition>
</AnnotatedOnly>


#### Static Semantics

<MarginText>13</MarginText>
<p>The effect of Divide is as follows. The value of Quotient is Quotient{"_"}Type(Dividend/Divisor). The value of Remainder is Remainder{"_"}Type(Intermediate), where Intermediate is the difference between Dividend and the product of Divisor and Quotient; this result is computed exactly. <br /></p>


#### Implementation Requirements

<MarginText>14</MarginText>
<p>Decimal.Max{"_"}Decimal{"_"}Digits shall be at least 18.<br /></p>

<MarginText>15</MarginText>
<p>Decimal.Max{"_"}Scale shall be at least 18.<br /></p>

<MarginText>16</MarginText>
<p>Decimal.Min{"_"}Scale shall be at most 0. <br /></p>

<AnnotatedOnly>
<MarginText>17</MarginText>
<Admonition type="aarm" aarm="note">
NOTE   The effect of division yielding a quotient with control over rounding versus truncation is obtained by applying either the function attribute Quotient{"_"}Type'Round or the conversion Quotient{"_"}Type to the expression Dividend/Divisor. <br />
</Admonition>
</AnnotatedOnly>

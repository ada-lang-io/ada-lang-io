<MarginText>1</MarginText>
<p>[An <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-5/AA-5.3#S0175">if_statement</a></code> selects for execution at most one of the enclosed <code>sequences{"_"}of{"_"}statements</code>, depending on the (truth) value of one or more corresponding <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-4/AA-4.5#S0150">condition</a></code>s.] <br /></p>

#### Dynamic Semantics

<MarginText>5/3</MarginText>
<MarginInfo items={["AI05-0264-1"]} />
<p>For the execution of an <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-5/AA-5.3#S0175">if_statement</a></code>, the <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-4/AA-4.5#S0150">condition</a></code> specified after <b>if</b>, and any <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-4/AA-4.5#S0150">condition</a></code>s specified after <b>elsif</b>, are evaluated in succession (treating a final <b>else</b> as <b>elsif</b> True <b>then</b>), until one evaluates to True or all <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-4/AA-4.5#S0150">condition</a></code>s are evaluated and yield False. If a <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-4/AA-4.5#S0150">condition</a></code> evaluates to True, then the corresponding <code><a href="/docs/arm/AA-5/AA-5.1#S0166">sequence_of_statements</a></code> is executed; otherwise, none of them is executed. <br /></p>

<AnnotatedOnly>
<MarginText>5.a</MarginText>
<Admonition type="aarm" aarm="ramification">
<b></b>The part about all evaluating to False can't happen if there is an <b>else</b>, since that is herein considered equivalent to <b>elsif</b> True <b>then</b>. <br />
</Admonition>
</AnnotatedOnly>

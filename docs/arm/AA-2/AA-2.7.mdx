<MarginText>1</MarginText>
<p>A <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.7#S0018">comment</a></code> starts with two adjacent hyphens and extends up to the end of the line. <br /></p>


#### Syntax

<MarginText>2</MarginText>

<CodeBlock>
<code>comment</code><a id="S0018"></a><code> ::= </code>--{"{"}<em>non_end_of_line_</em><code>character</code>{"}"}<br />

</CodeBlock>
<MarginText>3</MarginText>
<p class="Indented2">A <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.7#S0018">comment</a></code> may appear on any line of a program. <br /></p>


#### Static Semantics

<MarginText>4</MarginText>
<p>The presence or absence of <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.7#S0018">comment</a></code>s has no influence on whether a program is legal or illegal. Furthermore, <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.7#S0018">comment</a></code>s do not influence the meaning of a program; their sole purpose is the enlightenment of the human reader. <br /></p>
